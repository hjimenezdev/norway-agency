ROUTES

What can you do?

Welcome to Norway, a land of majestic landscapes and scenic wonders, where every turn in the road reveals a breathtaking vista. Whether you embark on the iconic coastal journey along the fjords, venture into the Arctic Circle to witness the dancing Northern Lights, or explore the mountainous terrain with its cascading waterfalls, Norway's routes promise an unforgettable adventure.


Each route unfolds a chapter of Norway's natural beauty, from the dramatic cliffs of the Atlantic Road to the serene serpentine roads of the Trollstigen. Get ready to traverse through a tapestry of fjords, mountains, and charming villages, as Norway's routes beckon you to explore the essence of this enchanting Scandinavian country.


Embark on a road trip through Norway and immerse yourself in a symphony of landscapes. From the winding coastal roads along the dramatic fjords to the enchanting Arctic highways, each route unveils a unique story of nature's grandeur.

Norwegian Splendors

Day 1

Oslo's Cultural Marvels

Oslo's Cultural Marvels beckon with a captivating blend of artistic wonders and historical treasures. Immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit of Norway's capital as you explore the renowned Munch Museum, stroll through the sculpted beauty of Vigeland Park, and journey through seafaring history at the Viking Ship Museum. Discover the essence of Oslo's cultural richness within the walls of these iconic institutions

Day 2

Bryggen Beauty and Fjord Exploration

Embark on a scenic train journey to Bergen, a city with a rich maritime history. Wander through the UNESCO-listed Bryggen Wharf, where Hanseatic buildings showcase the city's medieval past. Sample local delicacies at the lively Fish Market and consider exploring the vibrant Bergenhus Fortress. In the afternoon, let the stunning Norwegian fjords captivate you on a relaxing fjord cruise, providing an unforgettable perspective of Norway's natural beauty.

Day 3

Scenic Flåm and the Flam Railway

Venture towards Flåm, a charming village nestled amid breathtaking landscapes. Experience the awe-inspiring Flåm Railway, winding through majestic mountains and offering panoramic views of waterfalls and valleys. Explore the village, perhaps visiting the Flåm Railway Museum to delve deeper into the history of this engineering marvel. Consider a leisurely fjord cruise to fully appreciate the serenity and grandeur of the surrounding scenery.

Day 4

Geiranger's Natural Splendor

Journey to Geiranger, a UNESCO World Heritage Site enveloped in natural wonders. Explore the iconic Geirangerfjord, where cascading waterfalls and steep cliffs create a mesmerizing landscape. Ascend the Eagle Road for breathtaking panoramic views, and consider taking a hike to secluded viewpoints for a deeper connection with this majestic fjord. Round off your day with a tranquil boat trip, allowing you to fully absorb the tranquility and beauty of this Norwegian gem.

Day 5

Trondheim's Timeless Treasures

Head to Trondheim, a city steeped in history. Marvel at the grandeur of Nidaros Cathedral, a Gothic masterpiece and a pilgrimage site for centuries. Wander through the charming Bakklandet district, characterized by its well-preserved wooden houses and cobblestone streets. Dive into Trondheim's past at the Archbishop's Palace Museum, providing insights into the city's cultural and historical evolution.

Day 6

Alesund's Architectural Wonders

Conclude your journey in Ålesund, renowned for its Art Nouveau architecture. Stroll through the city's picturesque streets, where vibrant buildings showcase exquisite details. Explore the Ålesund Aquarium, offering an educational and entertaining experience with its diverse marine life. Cap off your visit at Aksla viewpoint, where panoramic vistas of the surrounding islands and sea create a fitting conclusion to your exploration. Conclude your Norwegian adventure with a delightful seafood dinner in one of Ålesund's charming restaurants, savoring the flavors of the region.

Norwegian Adventure

Day 1

Oslo's Historical Tapestry

Begin your exploration in Oslo by visiting the Akershus Fortress, a medieval castle with panoramic views of the city and fjord. Dive into Norwegian maritime history at the Fram Museum, showcasing polar exploration. Experience modern Oslo at the striking Oslo Opera House where you can walk on the roof for stunning views. Conclude the day with a visit to Mathallen Oslo, a food hall featuring a variety of Norwegian delicacies.

Day 2

Arctic Adventures in Tromsø

Fly to Tromsø, located in the Arctic Circle. Visit the iconic Arctic Cathedral and explore the Polaria Aquarium to learn about Arctic marine life. Take a cable car to Mount Storsteinen for breathtaking views of Tromsø and the surrounding islands. In the evening, chase the Northern Lights on a guided tour or enjoy a cozy evening in one of Tromsø's charming cafes.

Day 3

Lofoten's Coastal Beauty

Fly to Svolvær in the Lofoten Islands, known for its dramatic landscapes. Explore the picturesque fishing villages of Henningsvær and Å, surrounded by rugged mountains. Hike to the top of Reinebringen for panoramic views of the fjords and the sea. Visit the Lofoten Stockfish Museum to learn about the traditional fishing industry.

Day 4

Tranquil Trondheim Waterways

Travel to Trondheim and explore the Nidelva River by taking a relaxing boat tour. Visit the Old Town Bridge, providing picturesque views of colorful warehouses along the riverbanks. Discover the Ringve Museum of Musical History, showcasing a vast collection of musical instruments. Stroll along the river, enjoying the tranquility of Trondheim's waterside.

Day 5

Alpine Adventure in Jotunheimen

Head to Jotunheimen National Park, Norway's largest national park. Hike to Galdhøpiggen, the highest peak in Northern Europe, for breathtaking views of glaciers and mountains. Explore the Besseggen Ridge, offering stunning vistas of emerald lakes and rocky terrain. Conclude the day with a relaxing evening at a mountain cabin, immersing yourself in the peaceful alpine surroundings.

Day 6

Coastal Wonders of Stavanger

Conclude your journey in Stavanger, located on the southwest coast. Explore the Lysefjord by taking a boat tour, passing by towering cliffs and the famous Pulpit Rock. Hike to Preikestolen for a mesmerizing view over the fjord. Visit the Stavanger Cathedral and stroll through the charming Old Town. Conclude your trip with a seafood feast at the lively Fish Market.

Norwegian Odyssey

Day 1

Oslo's Cultural Kaleidoscope

Start your journey by immersing yourself in Oslo's art scene at the Munch Museum, dedicated to the works of Edvard Munch. Explore the Oslo Botanical Garden for a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city. Visit the National Gallery to see iconic Norwegian paintings, including "The Scream." Wrap up the day with a leisurely walk around the charming neighborhood of Grünerløkka, known for its trendy shops and cafes.

Day 2

Ålesund's Coastal Elegance

Fly to Ålesund, a town renowned for its Art Nouveau architecture. Climb the Aksla Viewpoint for panoramic views of the town and surrounding islands. Explore the Jugendstilsenteret, a museum dedicated to Art Nouveau. Take a boat trip to the nearby islands of Giske and Godøy for scenic landscapes and historical sites.

Day 3

Arctic Adventure in Kirkenes

Travel to Kirkenes, located in the Arctic Circle. Experience a dog sledding adventure through the snowy landscapes, led by huskies. Visit the Snow Hotel to see intricate ice sculptures and spend the night in a unique ice room. If visiting during winter, embark on a Northern Lights safari for a chance to witness the aurora borealis.

Day 4

Molde's Coastal Tranquility

Head to Molde, often referred to as the "City of Roses". Stroll through the Molde International Rose Garden, a beautiful collection of over 150 varieties. Visit the Molde International Jazz Festival if your visit coincides with the event. Explore Molde's Maritime Museum to learn about the town's seafaring history.

Day 5

Arctic Wildlife Discovery in Tromsø

Return to Tromsø and explore the Polar Museum to learn about the region's polar exploration history. Take a wildlife-watching tour to spot whales and seals in the surrounding fjords. Visit the Tromsø Arctic-Alpine Botanic Garden to discover the unique flora adapted to Arctic conditions.

Day 6

Stavanger's Coastal Trails

Conclude your journey in Stavanger, known for its coastal beauty. Hike the coastal trail to reach the secluded Høgsfjord, surrounded by towering cliffs. Explore the Stavanger Cathedral and the Stavanger Maritime Museum to delve into the city's maritime heritage. End your trip with a scenic drive to the picturesque village of Jørpeland and a visit to the idyllic Refsnesstranda Beach.
